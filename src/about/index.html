Mission

Orlando Devs is an Orlando-area wide community focused on growing and supporting developers at all levels to build their careers in Central Florida. Through the running of regular networking events, training sessions, hackathons, and collaboration events, Orlando Devs works to support developers in skill development and community-building. As a member of Innovate Orlando, the greater technology community of the Orlando area, Orlando Devs fosters collaboration between technologists, entrepreneurs, enterprise businesses, academia, and more. With a keen focus on growing diversity in the technology ecosystem, Orlando Devs supports programming that builds the talent pipeline for women and minorities in technology.

Orlando Devs gives back to the community through mentorship, hosted events, and volunteerism to grow the tech community of Orlando. Orlando Devs is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.

The mission of ODevs is about lifting up the Orlando community through developer focused events, networking, and education. This also includes supporting other meetups that share in these values and mission.

Benefits of the program

  • Cover meetup fees
  • Guaranteed promotion via newsletter, slack, social
  • Assist with booking venues and fees if applicable
  • Reimbursement for food and drink
  • When available, provide on-site coordinator/volunteer services
  • When appropriate assistance with swag
  • Access to ODev sponsors
  • Provide event planning services for larger events
  • Backup organizers to ensure continuity
  • Succession planning when organizers step down
  • Assistance with presentation materials, i.e. PowerPoint slides
  • Allow use of our 501(c)3 non-profit status for program-specific fundraising
  • Allow use of our 501(c)3 non-profit status for discounts

Responsibilities of being an organizer

  • Programming ideas and date selection
  • Book speakers and provide the speaker information via the speaker form to the ODevs Program Coordinator
  • Providing your preferred dates to the ODevs Program Coordinator, two dates in advance for meetups (Preferred dates are not guaranteed)
  • Provide your preferred meetup location to the ODevs Program Coordinator, Credo, or Full Sail (Preferred location is not guaranteed)
  • Attend and run your events (let us know if you need a backup)
  • Add ODevs as admin on any sites
  • Include ODev sponsors on meetup presentations/materials
  • Abide by meetup location guidelines including leaving the space clean
  • You or a designated community member take pictures at events and upload them to the ODevs Google Photo Album
  • Review all ODev slides at meetups if the ODevs Program Coordinator is not available
  • Abide by and agree to the ODevs Code of Conduct
  • If organizers wish to obtain sponsorship for a specific event, they may do so. However, if they are seeking general sponsorships for their organization, they should go through ODevs.
